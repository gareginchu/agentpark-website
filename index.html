About AgentPark

Where Startups Scale, Communities Connect, and Ideas Become Impact

What We Do

Building the Future of AI in Armenia

A Launchpad for Innovation

AgentPark functions as both an AI accelerator and incubator, nurturing local startups with strategic support in technology, business development, and consulting. Backed by financial investment and an experienced leadership team.

A Knowledge & Talent Magnet

With strong ties to Armenia's leading universities, such as the Faculty of Applied Mathematics at Yerevan State University. The Park hosted its first AI Summer School in July 2025, with ongoing academic collaborations.

A Cross-Sector Platform

Building bridges across sectors — integrating healthcare, agriculture, design, and cultural institutions into its innovation framework. Making AI more accessible and relevant to everyday challenges.

A Cultural & Creative Nexus

More than a tech hub — a platform for art and design, where creative minds collaborate with technical experts to redefine the boundaries of expression, interaction, and intelligence.
